Our Heritage

A Legacy of Excellence

For over two decades, Beirut has been the cornerstone of authentic Lebanese cuisine in San Pedro Garza García. Our journey began with a simple vision: to bring the warmth and flavors of Lebanon to Mexico, creating an unforgettable dining experience that honors tradition while embracing innovation.

Every dish tells a story of heritage, crafted with recipes passed down through generations and elevated with contemporary culinary artistry. Our commitment to excellence has made us not just a restaurant, but a cultural destination where memories are created and traditions are celebrated.

The Shisha Lounge Experience

Step into our exclusive Shisha Lounge, where aromatic clouds of premium tobacco blend with sophisticated ambiance. Our master mixologists craft unique flavor combinations using only the finest imported tobaccos from Dubai and Lebanon.

Whether you prefer classic double apple or adventurous fusion blends, each session is a ceremonial experience, complemented by our selection of exotic teas and traditional Lebanese sweets.
